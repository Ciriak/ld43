ELDER LOOP

The dungeon of the chosen child

PLAY
HOW TO PLAY

In ‘Elder loop’ you play an older wizard named Demange The goal of the game is to slain as much enemies as you can, each monster will increase one of the 4 main stats you have.

You can’t go higher than 5 for a given stat. Each enemy you slain will give you some point for your score and some Sanity … More you kill more you will lose sanity, when the bar is full the monster will be harder to kill.

HOW TO PLAY

Use WASD to move around, use mouth click to cast a spell. The spell will aim where your cursor is.

At any time when you have 5 of a given stat you will be able to sacrifice these to gain a bonus for your scoring. When you have slain all the monsters in the dungeon the game will restart, you will keep all of your stats, points etc … The madness will never end !

Sometimes you will find some relics on the ground, they will give you 5 points of your stats but the enemy will be stronger !
